2024/2025 Season

Pre-Season

Following last seasons quardruple, the goals for this season largely follow on, aiming to retain all domestic trophies, but also reach the latter stages of the Champions League.

Formation

Don't fix what isn't broken? Last seasons dominance surely means we keep to the same tactics as the previous season.
Although a secondary tactic will be experimented with in cup games. This is because FM24's AI will alter opposition tactics to counter yours. To minimise that, a backup tactic is always useful to fall back on.
For this season, as a backup tactic I'll be looking to integrate a classic 4-4-2 into the team, with a third 4-2-4 formation to allow the inclusion of the attacking wingers.
These formations will be trialed in friendly and low risk cup games against weaker oppositions.

Summer Transfers

Transfer interest in first team players will likely be high in the summer transfer window. Whilst I'll be looking to keep the squad together, if a big money offer comes in I would consider it, and re-invest in youth.
After the signings of Dybala and Schlager, a focus on home grown talent will be the priority this season, to comply with Champions League and Premier League regulations.
